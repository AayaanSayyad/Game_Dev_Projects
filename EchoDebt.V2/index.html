How to play

Short and sweet — enough to survive Teal Rift. The rest you learn by orbiting.

Controls

  • A / D or ← / → — spin around the ring
  • W / S or ↑ / ↓ — hop to an inner or outer ring (near the rim)
  • 1 – 2 — use a held candy from your slots
  • SPACE — character super (if you have one)
  • I — peek at inventory

The goal

Survive until the timer runs out. You have a few lives — when they hit zero, the run ends.

Debt is how many clones are on the board. Taking a hit usually adds more debt, so staying clean keeps the fight manageable.

What to watch for

  • Clones

    Little copies that orbit the same rings as you. Bumping one costs a life — but that clone pops too, so the board gets a tiny bit cleaner.

  • Lasers

    They telegraph first (a faint line or ring flash), then fire. Move off the path before it locks in. Later worlds get meaner sweeps.

  • Rocks & debris

    Asteroids drift in; shattered pieces can still hurt. Give them space, or clear them with the right candy.

  • Candies

    Powerups sitting on the rings. Grab them as you pass. Some use right away; others go into your slots for later. Read the popup the first time you find a new one.

One tip

Hop rings to dodge, don’t only spin. Outer rings feel roomier early — use that until you’re comfortable.
