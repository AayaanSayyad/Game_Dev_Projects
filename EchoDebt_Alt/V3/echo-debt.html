HOW TO PLAY

  • Tap, click, or press Space to reverse your orbit
  • Dodge the red pulses fired from the planet's core
  • Collect every node to clear the level — chain them quickly to build a score multiplier
  • Miss a pulse and you rewind — a red Echo spawns and repeats what you just did (and your multiplier resets)
  • Watch for the blink: that's where you'll reappear, a beat later
  • Echoes block pulses for you... but touch one and you rewind again
  • Press W/↑ or S/↓ (or the OUT/IN buttons) to hop off the ring — anything sitting on it can't touch you mid-jump
  • Relief nodes give you a power to hold — tap the orb below (or press E) to use it whenever you want
  • Higher levels bring drifting hazards and pulses that hunt you
  • Stack too many Echoes and it's game over. Press Esc anytime to pause
