# Echo Debt (Cotton)

Orbit. Flip. Dodge the clones your deaths leave behind.

## Play

```bash
python3 -m http.server 8080 --directory .
```

Open `http://localhost:8080` — click once for audio.

## Modes

- **Campaign** — 10 levels, exponential heat  
- **Endless** — escalation every 15s  

Each level starts with a short **preview pause**. Planet look changes every 2 levels.

## Movement

**A/D (←/→)** move left/right **as you see it on screen** (not fixed clockwise).  
**W/S** switch rings · **Space** hop on rims · **1/2** held powerups

## Candies

Two mystery candies per level — identical look until you bump one. Then a popup explains (buff or curse).

**New hold:** Rampage — invincible and crush clones you touch.
